Follow all rules strictly.

STRUCTURE

Avoid abstract visuals
Make every scene understandable in 1–2 seconds

small figures

Forbidden:
faces
detailed people
emotional expressions
character-focused shots

Rule: if the scene works without humans, do not include humans.

No paper texture

clean cinematic historical illustration, slightly simplified, semi-realistic, smooth shading, sharp edges, no brush texture, no paint effect, full-frame, no text, no borders
